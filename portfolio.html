About Us


A very warm welcome to YASHASHRI POLYPLAST for a complete solution of your uPVC windows , cabins, doors and partitions etc . Established in the year 2003 & still growing in terms of innovation & quality with having most modern manufacturing facilities in our factory.
We are situated in Shiroda industrial Estate, Shiroda, Goa India, & operating in others cities and states of India with an unblemished track record.We are a factory, which deals in everything that comes under uPVC umbrella & works with total commitment to help turn your dreams & ideas into solid images whether it’s about beautifying your home with artistically designed fabrication, YASHASHRI POLYPLAST are specialists in the design, manufacture, and installation uPVC Windows & Doors, Cabins, partition etc.
We feel pleased to state that under this banner we have executed & successfully completed various projects ranging from Luxury Villas & Housing Complexes to Educational Institutions for public & private sectors alike.
YASHASHRI POLYPLAST factory is designed to take full advantage of modern mass- production techniques to ensure highest levels of accuracy combined with high outputs at optimum cost. All design-work is generated in-house and all installation-works are handled by expert team of site engineers and fabricators. This detailed integration of personnel and plant is a guarantee of the company’s capacity to deliver even the most complex orders in a timely and efficient manner, and to the standards specified by the Client.
We believe in creating long-term relationships with our clients, and hence cater to their diverse needs & requirements. We have garnered the patronage of high profile clients. Our clients are from various sectors like hotels, reputed builders for official and residential complexes, banks, government sectors and others. We offer cost effective solutions that are tailored to meet their requirements.
